UK Box Office Analysis Portfolio
30 May–1 June 2025

Project Overview

This portfolio presents an analysis of the BFI Weekend Box Office report for 30 May–1 June 2025. Data was cleaned and prepared in Excel, and visualized with Power BI to extract actionable insights for film distributors and stakeholders.

Files & Resources

  • Cleaned Excel Data: data/bfi-weekend-box-office-report-2025-05-30-06-01-2.xlsx
  • Power BI Dashboard: box_office_dashboard.pbix
  • Portfolio Summary (PDF/Word): docs/portfolio_summary.pdf
  • Visuals & Screenshots: visuals/ folder
  • README: docs/README.md

Key Insights

Top Film: “Lilo & Stitch” led the weekend box office with £6.1M, confirming strong family demand.
Country Representation: UK films were the most numerous, reflecting a robust domestic industry.
Outliers: “Bogancloch” achieved a high site average despite limited release, highlighting the value of niche targeting.
Market Diversity: The box office featured blockbusters, indie films, re-releases, and event screenings.
